This example shows how raw OSM data is transformed into structured geometry and integrated with real-world terrain.

- Raw data: road network and geographic features from OSM
- Processing: procedural generation of street layouts and spatial structure
- Result: clean, scalable geometry aligned with terrain and satellite context
